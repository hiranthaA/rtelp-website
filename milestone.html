Milestones

The following milestones are used to achieve the goal of developing the EPMS. The project will be evaluated out of 100 marks to be achieved at the end of the year. Different percentages are allocated for each milestone achievement out of the 100 marks. Team has met the all deadlines and produced the relevant documentations and the required presentations of the EPMS project.

Deliverables

PROPOSAL

Project proposal compromises with two assessments as Project Proposal Document and Project Proposal Presentation. Project Proposal document is the initial document that demarcate the initial requirements of the project activities. The proposal document describes all the techniques to be used in the research. Marks for the proposal document were 5 out of 100. Proposal Documents was supposed to submit on or before 27 th of March, 2017. Proposal Presentation was conducted to evaluate the research idea and methodology for the proposed solution. On the 6 th of April, 2017.

SRS PRESENTATION

Importance of the Software Requirements Specification (SRS) document is to provide all the necessary information which are related to the requirements of the related system. Detailed description of what the system is about and how to use the system will be included in the SRS document for all the users who are willing to interact with the system. Allocated marks for the SRS Document were 6 out of 100. SRS Presentation was evaluated on the 2 nd Of May, 2017

PROGRESS 01

Progress Presentation – 1 was an assessment which gives a fresh, unbiased view of the project. Half of the project work supposed to be completed at the time of the presentation. This Presentation helps to identify potential for improvement, provides realistic and results-oriented recommendations. 10 marks out of 100 were allocated to this particular presentation. Presentation was held on the 14th of July, 2017.

PROGRESS 02

Progress Presentation – 2 was mainly conducted to evaluate the completion of the research work of each component of the students. At least 90% of the research work should be done prior to this presentation. Completion of all the components along with the integration would be the ideal situation. Out 0f 100 marks, 16 were allocated to this particular assessment. Presentation was evaluated on the 25 th of September, 2017.

FINAL PRESENTATION

All the research components of the project have to be completed and integrated prior to Final Assessment. Completed product has to be tested and finally should evaluate the accuracy results. 10 marks out of 100 will be allocated for the assessment and it will held on 27th of November to 03rd of December, 2017.

VIVA

Viva will be conducted on the same day in which final assessment will be evaluated. Questions will be asked from the students individually regarding their work on the research component in order to clarify their own contribution towards the components. 15 marks will be given to the Viva out of 100 marks for the whole research project.
